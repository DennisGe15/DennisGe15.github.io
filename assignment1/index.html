Question 8

After interacting with Min's website, I found that the overall clean design and intuitive navigation of the site make it easy for users to understand how to interact as soon as they enter the site. The site's interactions also have some visual feedback, as when the user hovers over a clickable button, the button will indicate that it can be interacted with through a color change or a slight animation. However, some areas of the site should be optimized, for example, the only interaction modes on the site are the basic mouse click and scroll wheel. This design can make users feel bored and drained during repeated visits. If one or two new interaction modes could be added to the site, it would entice users to stay longer and use the site again and again.
